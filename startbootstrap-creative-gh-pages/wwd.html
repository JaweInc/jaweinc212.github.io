Reputable Solicitors is a leading Law Firm in Nigeria with a strong reputation for competence and integrity in service delivery.

What We Do

Our practice covers the representation of clients in commercial, civil and criminal disputes and advising on transactional and corporate matters. We work with a non-rigid practice group structure, in which our lawyers are not necessarily restricted to one practice area and we encourage our lawyers to take on a variety of work and build capacity across our practice areas. We are passionate about the deveplopment and growth of nigeria and the communities in which we work


Our practice groups are the:

  1. Commercial Law Practice Group
  2. Dispute Resolution Practice Group
  3. Legislative and Media Practice Group
  4. Energy, Technology and Infrastructure Practice Group
  5. Regulatory Compliance and Business Advisory Practice Group
