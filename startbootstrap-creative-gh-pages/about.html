About Us

Reputable Solicitors is the exclusive member of ‘PraeLegal’ in Nigeria, a global Network of independent Law Firms with membership in over 120 countries spanning five continents of Africa, Asia, Europe, North America and South America. Our exclusive membership of ‘PraeLegal’ gives us the unique advantage of being able to effectively deal with cross border matters using the contacts afforded by the ‘PraeLegal’ Network, in providing our Clients with a seamless blend of local and international expertise. The Firm is also registered with the Lagos Court of Arbitration and Nigerian-Belgian Chamber of Commerce as a corporate member.

Our Firm is a leading multi-service Law Firm in Nigeria with a strong reputation for integrity in service delivery. We are a Limited Partnership registered under the Laws of Lagos State. Our practice is driven and bound by our core values of Diligence, Integrity, Proficiency and Professional etiquette, which uncompromisingly shape all our relationships with clients, courts, colleagues and regulatory agencies.

Our vision is to be the most influential Firm making the most positive impact on the lives and businesses of clients. In advising and representing our clients, we rely on our invaluable experience and knowledge of the complex Nigerian business and legal landscape, which gives us a unique appreciation of and approach to matters and shapes the manner in which we deal with our clients’ challenges. We always aim at understanding the commercial and technical aspects of our clients’ businesses and the markets in which they operate, as this enables us to ensure that we provide the most useful and cost effective solutions to them.
